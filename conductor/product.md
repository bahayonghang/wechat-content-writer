# Product Guide: WeChat Content Writer

This document outlines the product vision, target audience, goals, and core principles for the WeChat Content Writer, a professional Claude Code plugin.

## 1. Initial Concept

The project is a sophisticated content creation plugin designed for professional WeChat official account publishers. It combines AI-powered research capabilities with objective, technical writing standards to help create high-quality articles efficiently.

## 2. Target Audience

The primary users of this tool are professionals who require depth, accuracy, and efficiency in their content creation workflow:

-   **Professional WeChat Official Account Publishers:** Individuals and teams focusing on creating in-depth, high-quality technical or academic content.
-   **Technical Bloggers and Academic Researchers:** Users who need to frequently cite literature and data, requiring a streamlined research-to-writing process.
-   **Corporate Content Marketing Teams:** Teams that need to maintain a high standard of professionalism and consistency in their published materials.

## 3. Core Goals & Objectives

The project aims to deliver a comprehensive solution by focusing on three key pillars:

-   **Efficiency Enhancement:** Automate the tedious aspects of technical and academic research, significantly reducing the time required for content creation.
-   **Quality Assurance:** Ensure all generated content is professional, well-structured, and free of common "AI-flavored" language, thereby enhancing credibility.
-   **Workflow Integration:** Provide a seamless, end-to-end workflow from literature/PDF analysis to a ready-to-publish draft, all within the Claude Code environment.

## 4. Core Functional Modules

To achieve its goals, the project is built upon the following core functional modules:

-   **Intelligent Literature Search:** Integrated search capabilities for academic databases like arXiv and Google Scholar to find relevant and timely sources.
-   **In-depth Document/PDF Analysis:** Advanced parsing and comprehension of complex documents and PDFs to extract key information, arguments, and data.
-   **Structured Article Generation:** Automated creation of articles that are well-organized, adhere to WeChat's formatting conventions, and align with a professional writing style.

## 5. Unique Value Proposition (UVP) & Success Metrics

The project's success and unique value are defined by:

-   **Time-to-Publish Reduction (Efficiency):** Drastically shortening the cycle from topic discovery to a finalized, typeset draft ready for WeChat.
-   **Brand Credibility (Quality):** Establishing a reputation for high-trust technical and academic content that stands out from generic AI-generated articles.
-   **Seamless Developer Experience (UX):** Perfect integration with the developer's native CLI workflow in Claude Code, eliminating context switching.

## 6. Guiding Principle: Writing Style

The cornerstone of this project is its commitment to a specific writing style, characterized by:

-   **Strict Objectivity:** A neutral tone that avoids sensational or emotionally charged language, which is key to eliminating "AI flavor."
-   **Data-Driven and Evidence-Based:** A strong emphasis on factual support and proper citation of literature and data sources.
-   **Professional Terminology:** The precise and appropriate use of technical terms to meet the expectations of a specialized audience.
